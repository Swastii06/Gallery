Gallery of the Indian History

AKBAR

He is the greatest monarch of the Mughal dynasty.

RANI PADMAVATI

Padmini, also known as Padmavati,
was a 13th-14th century queen of the Mewar kingdom.

CHANDRAGUPTA MAURYA

He was a ruler of Iron age South Asia
and the founder of Maurya dynasty.

MAHARANA PRATAP

Pratap Singh I, popularly known as Maharana Pratap,
was a Hindu Rajput king of Mewar.

ASHOKA

Ashoka the Great was the third emporer
of the Maurya Empire of the Indian subcontinent.

RANI OF JHANSI

AKA Rani Lakshmibai, was the maharani of
princely state of Jhansi from 1843 to 1853.

CHHATRAPATI SHIVAJI

Shivaji I was an Indian ruler and
a member of Bhonsle Maratha clan.

TIPU SULTAN

AKA Tiger of Mysore, was the Indian Muslim ruler
of the kingdom of Mysore.
